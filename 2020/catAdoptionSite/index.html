This is a site built for those who cant keep their pet because of leaving the country, due to health, or financial reasons.

We will help find them a new loving home! We will not let any pet out without confirming that they will have a good and sure home which can take care of them even if they get sick and dont abandon them. We also provide shelter for the pets until they can find a new family. Because of that sadly we can only welcome a restricted number of pets at a time. For further information and ways to contact us please check out the "About" part of the site!
