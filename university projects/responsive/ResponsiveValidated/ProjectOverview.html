About us

Colour IT is a small local company who develops and implements IT systems for a mostly private clientele. No matter the scope of a project we will do our utmost to realize your ideas.
Our developers are a tight-knit and dedicated group of colourful individuals who are always up for a challenge. Their top priority is to ensure that finished projects are of the highest quality and our clients are satisfied with their product.
We strive to put a smile on your face and a splash of colour in your day!
